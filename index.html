Rating System & Algorithms

Elo Rating System

Our primary rating system based on the Elo algorithm with anti-sandbag protection.

Formula: Ra' = Ra + K × (W - E)
  • Ra: Player's old rating
  • K: K-factor (varies by skill level)
  • W: Match result (1=win, 0=loss)
  • E: Expected score based on rating difference

K-Factor System

K-factor determines how much a rating can change per match.

Rating Range K-Factor
0 - 1200 32
1201 - 1600 24
1601 - 2000 16
2001+ 12

Anti-Sandbag Algorithm

Prevents rating manipulation by detecting unusual win patterns.

  • Detects: Wins against much higher rated players
  • Flags: Unusual scoring patterns
  • Adjusts: Rating changes for suspicious matches
  • Protects: Fair competition integrity
